Founded a web development and hosting company way back in 2003. Still operating it today.

Interesting portions of this job included dealing with email delivery to an enourmous double opt-in email list and reverse engineering affiliate network redirection schemes for deep linking. BFAds was acquired by Ebates in 2012.
